### 4. Create After-Hours Landing Pages

Here's a tactic most businesses miss: **dedicated after-hours landing pages**. When someone clicks your Google ad at 10 PM, they shouldn't land on a page that says "Call us during business hours." They should land on a page optimized for after-hours conversion.

### 5. Build Automated Nurture Sequences

Capturing a lead is step one. Converting them is the real goal. After-hours leads need a carefully timed follow-up sequence that starts immediately and continues until they book or opt out.

**Recommended after-hours nurture timeline:**

1. **Immediate:** Automated acknowledgment (SMS + email)
2. **Next morning (8-9 AM):** Personal call or email from your team
3. **Day 2:** Educational content related to their inquiry
4. **Day 4:** Social proof (testimonial or case study)
5. **Day 7:** Gentle follow-up with a time-limited offer
6. **Day 14:** Final check-in

This sequence works because it balances automation with personal touch. The lead gets instant gratification from the automated response, then human connection during business hours, then ongoing value that keeps your business top of mind.
